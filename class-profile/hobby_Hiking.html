I love spending time in the woods. I enjoy just walking down a trail or blazing my own, or maybe going really fast on a mountain bike. There is something spiritual and cleansing about nature that I am attracted to.

      The Appalachian Trail is my favorite trail in the United States; it spans from Maine to Georgia, and has some of the best views around (it follows the Appalachian mountain peaks). My next favorite would probably be the mid-state trail, which cuts Pennsylvania in half and spans 325 miles. There are many good books about trails and nature that are worth a read.
